# Scoring Model

Score each category from `1` to `5`.

## Categories

- `strategic_fit`: aligns with roadmap, brand, and reusable assets
- `execution_fit`: realistically shippable with current capabilities
- `upside`: cash, reputation, users, or leverage if it works
- `competition_position`: odds of standing out versus the field
- `time_sensitivity`: urgency versus available focus
- `downside_risk`: low score means high hidden cost or risk

## Recommended interpretation

- `22-30`: strong pursue
- `16-21`: pursue only if current pipeline allows
- `10-15`: defer unless there is unusual strategic value
- `<10`: drop

## Override rules

Override the numeric score if:

- deadline is impossible
- reward depends on infrastructure or access you do not have
- the opportunity is strategically perfect even if crowded
- a low-cash opportunity creates a high-value reusable asset

When overriding, explain the reason in one sentence.
