# Opportunity Evaluation Workflow

## Inputs to gather

- official listing or offer page
- deadline and time sensitivity
- reward, revenue, or strategic upside
- sponsor quality and reputation
- required deliverable quality bar
- proof of demand or ecosystem relevance
- hidden dependencies: KYC, infra, distribution, legal, paid APIs, design, chain data

## Questions to answer

### 1. Strategic fit

- does this strengthen the current roadmap
- does it create a reusable asset
- does it unlock distribution, reputation, users, or technical leverage

### 2. Execution fit

- can the team finish with current skills and tools
- is the work mostly research, shipping, integrations, or content
- does it require external dependencies we do not control

### 3. Reward quality

- is the prize or upside meaningful after competition and effort
- is the value only cash, or also relationship / signal / portfolio

### 4. Timing

- is the deadline realistic
- is there a better time to do the same work later
- will it distract from a more important compounding project

### 5. Downside

- what happens if we lose
- what happens if we win but the opportunity creates maintenance burden
- what reputational risk exists if the sponsor or deliverable quality is weak

## Required output behavior

- be direct
- prefer evidence over vibes
- separate facts from inference
- call out missing inputs explicitly
- recommend one next step, not five equal options
